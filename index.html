When I am not coding, you can find me composing music or at an open mic night with my acoustic guitar. I also love video games, but unfortunately have had to reduce my play-time to completing one video game a year. I only play video games with an ending as I simply do not have time for a game that never ends (how does anyone come to think of it?). If you want to know more. Just ask.
